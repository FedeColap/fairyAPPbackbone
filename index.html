Welcome to the world of fairy tales!

This app has been created with love to help you discover new stories, read and even listen to some of them.
There are thousands of tales worldwide, and of course it's not possible to collect all of them -not yet, at least-
but here you can find a good starting point.
The "info" section will give you a brief description of any tale you have in mind.
In the "read" and "listen" sections, the Fairy of Internet will choose a random story for you :-)
Lastly, the "list" button will give you a selection of some audiotales, written by the Grimm Brothers (this time, you pick!)

May you be inspired and enjoy the dive in this magical world!
